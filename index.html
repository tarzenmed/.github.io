Cardiology

Cardiology is the branch of medicine that deals with the heart and blood vessels. Cardiologists diagnose and treat a wide range of heart conditions, including:

i) Coronary artery disease (CAD)                ii) Heartvfailure
iii) Arrhythmias (irregular heartbeats)      iv) Valvular heart disease
v) Congenital heart defects                        vi) Heart infections
vii) Heart muscle disease

Neurology

Neurology is the branch of medicine that deals with the nervous system, which includes the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. Neurologists diagnose and treat a wide range of neurological disorders, including:

i)Stroke                                           ii)Alzheimer's disease
iii)Parkinson's disease                 iv)Multiple sclerosis
v)Epilepsy                                      vi)Headaches
vii)Nerve damage

Hepatology

Hepatology is a specialized field of medicine that focuses on the study and management of the liver, gallbladder and related structures, as well as their associated diseases and disorders.

i)Liver Disease & Transplantation             ii)Hepatitis
iii)NAFLD & NASH                                        iv)Primary Biliary Cholangitis(PBC)
v)Diagnostic Procedures                            vi)Hepatocellular Carcinoma(HCC)
vii)Cirrhosis
